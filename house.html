House the Homeless

From a Bed for Tonight to a Home for Tomorrow

Everyone deserves a safe place to sleep. Our House the Homeless program supports emergency shelter beds, transitional housing, and partnerships that help people move into long-term, stable homes in Winnipeg.

Your daily dollar helps cover essentials that are often overlooked: clean bedding, basic furniture, bus passes to view apartments, and small move-in kits with dishes, towels, and cleaning supplies. These details turn an empty room into a place that feels like home.

Housing Support in Action

  • Emergency Shelter Support: Helping local shelters keep beds open and welcoming.
  • Transitional Housing: Supporting short-term housing for people moving off the streets.
  • Move-In Essentials: Providing starter kits so new tenants can settle in with dignity.

Housing is more than a roof—it is stability, safety, and the starting point for healing. Your one dollar a day helps someone take that crucial step.
