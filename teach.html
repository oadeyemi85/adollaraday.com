Teach the Homeless

Learning that Opens New Doors

Education is a powerful tool for breaking cycles of homelessness. Our Teach the Homeless program supports literacy classes, life-skills workshops, and job-readiness training for people who are rebuilding their lives in Winnipeg.

We partner with community educators and volunteers to offer small, welcoming classes: reading and writing support, basic computer skills, budgeting, cooking on a tight budget, and interview preparation. Your daily dollar helps provide materials, snacks, and safe spaces to learn.

Programs Your Dollar Supports

  • Literacy & GED Support: Helping adults strengthen reading, writing, and numeracy skills.
  • Life Skills Workshops: Budgeting, cooking, and everyday problem-solving for independent living.
  • Job Readiness: Resume help, interview practice, and basic digital skills for job searches.

When you give a dollar a day, you invest in someone’s future—knowledge that no one can take away.
