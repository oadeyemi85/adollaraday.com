Join Our Team

A Dollar A Day is powered by passionate volunteers who believe that small acts of kindness can create big change. Whether you want to help serve meals, organize clothing drives, support fundraising, or assist with community outreach, there’s a place for you on our team.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Meal preparation and serving
  • Clothing sorting and distribution
  • Shelter support and outreach
  • Fundraising and event support
  • Administrative and digital support

Why Volunteer With Us?

You’ll be part of a community that believes in dignity, compassion, and practical support for our neighbours. Volunteers receive training, support, and the chance to make a real impact in Winnipeg.
