Feed the Homeless

Food Bank Days and Hot Meal Programs

Hunger should never be part of anyone’s daily routine. Through our Feed the Homeless program, your daily dollar helps stock food bank shelves, fund hot meal services, and provide nutritious snacks for people living on the streets or in shelters across Winnipeg.

We focus on simple, hearty meals that bring comfort and stability: soups, stews, fresh bread, fruit, and warm drinks. Volunteers prepare and serve meals with respect, greeting each guest by name and offering a listening ear.

How Your Dollar Works Here

  • Food Bank Fridays: Weekly distribution of pantry staples and fresh produce in partnership with local food banks.
  • Street Outreach Meals: Portable meals delivered to encampments and areas where people gather.
  • Breakfast Before Shelter Check-Out: Simple breakfasts offered to shelter guests before they head out into the day.

One dollar may not seem like much, but when combined with hundreds of other daily donors, it becomes thousands of meals each month. That is the power of consistent, small generosity.
