Welcome to A Dollar A Day

Small gifts. Lasting change in Winnipeg.

A Dollar A Day is a grassroots charity based in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Our vision is simple: when many people give a little, no one is left behind. By donating just one dollar a day, you help provide meals, warm clothing, safe shelter, and learning opportunities for neighbours experiencing homelessness in our city.

We partner with local shelters, food banks, and community organizations to make sure every dollar is used wisely and transparently. Your daily dollar becomes hot soup on a winter night, a clean pair of socks, a bed for someone coming in from the cold, or a literacy class that opens new doors.

Recent Highlights

Food Bank Fridays

Thanks to daily donors, we now support a weekly “Food Bank Friday” in downtown Winnipeg, providing fresh produce, warm meals, and coffee to over 120 people each week.

Winter Warmth Drive

Our Clothe the Homeless program recently distributed winter jackets, gloves, and thermal socks ahead of the coldest months. Your one dollar a day helped fill those bags with dignity and warmth.

Pathway to Stability

Through House the Homeless and Teach the Homeless, we are supporting transitional housing and life-skills workshops that help people move from surviving to thriving.
